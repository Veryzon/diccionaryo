# Term: freedom

## v0.1.0 (DRAFT)

**Definition**  
The absence of externally imposed constraints on an agent’s action or state, within the defined bounds of a system’s rules.

**Domain**: political, philosophical  
**Forms**: freedom (noun), free (adj), freely (adv)

**Depends on**:  
- agent@1.0.0  
- constraint@1.0.0  
- system@1.0.0
